📘 How to Use TrackFit

🍽️

1. Daily Meal

Add your breakfast, lunch, and dinner calories. The app automatically calculates your daily food intake.

🏃‍♂️

2. Steps Tracking

Enter your daily steps. The system converts steps into distance (km) and calories burned.

💧

3. Water Intake

Set your daily water goal and track glasses consumed. Stay hydrated throughout the day.

🏋️

4. Workout

Follow daily workout plans. Calories burned are added automatically to your progress.

🔥

5. Daily Calories

View calories consumed, burned, remaining, and set daily goals. Includes manual entries.

📊

6. Progress & Reports

Track today’s progress and view monthly calorie & water graphs to understand trends.

💪 Stay Consistent

TrackFit resets data daily and helps you build healthy habits step by step.
